Who we are

RCG is a connection of talented young Rwandans
who are passionate about programming
who have decided to work together for great achievement

Our values

Critical thinking
collaboration and
problem solving
are our core values

Join us

If you want to join the connection of young
programmers who want to make Rwanda a hub of technology
it is free to join us but there are some condtions for
joining RCG.

Conditions for joinning RCG

  • You must be a university student doing programming
  • young people who are doing programming shortcourses also can join RCG
  • you must have a spirit of working in group because 80% of our projects are done in groups.

Benefits of being in RCG

In RCG we work in groups, we brainstorm together the solutions
to the problems in our society so being one of RCG will help you to develop problem solving skills
which is a neccessary skill that programmers need. Apart from that, we also help you to connect
with different people who have the same idea as yours so that you can collaborate on project and also
we connect you to young talented programmers around you.
